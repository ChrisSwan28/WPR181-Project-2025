The Falklands War

The Origin of The Falklands War

  • A long standing dispute between Argentina and Britian over the Falkland Islands
  • Argentin's military political group attempted to assert control
  • Britian sent a task force to reclaim the islands for themselves

The Key Events that took place during the War

  1. Argentina invaded the islands (April)
  2. British Navy task force sailed to reclaim territory and 2 ships HMS Sheffield and the Atlantic Conveyor sunk (May)
  3. British Navy landed on the east and advanced (May)
  4. Argentina Forces surrendered(June)

The Aftermath

The British won the war, which lasted 74 days. It reinforced Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher's administration and increased nationalism in the United Kingdom. The military in Argentina fell and democracy returned as a result of the defeat.
