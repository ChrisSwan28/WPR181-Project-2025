The Origin of The American Civil War

  • There was a deep disagreament over slavery where Southern States wanted it and Northern States opposed it
  • Disputes concerning State Rights vs federal authority
  • The election of Abraham Lincoln in 1860 led to Southern independancy
  • Thus 11 Southern States formed the confederacy

The Key Events that took place during the War

  1. A confederate attack on Fort Sumter (1861)
  2. The battle of Gettysburg which was a huge Union Vicotry (1863)
  3. Lincoln issued the emancipation proclamation which liberated slaves in rebel states (1863)
  4. The confederate general Robert E. Lee surrendered at Appomattox Court House (1865)

The Aftermath of the War

The most deadly war in American history where over 600 thousand American died led to the preservation of the Union and the abolition of slavery. The Reconstruction era aimed to reconcile the South and protect the rights of newly freed African Americans.
