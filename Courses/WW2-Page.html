The Origin of World War 2

  • The full timeline was from September 1st 1939 to June 22nd 1945
  • The Treaty of Versailles following World War 1 imposed harsh penalties on Germany forcing them to pay reporations, give up land and accept full blame for the war
  • The Great Depression was a serious economic struggle that helped the Nazi Party gain support
  • The league of Nations were created to maintain peace however they were too weak to enforce it which appeasemnet to Adolf Hitler
  • The Nazi-Soviet Pact was an agreement between Germany and the Soviet Union to split Poland which meant Hitler could inavde Poland without worrying about the Soviet Union

The Aftermath of World War 2

The war caused over 60 million deaths and it left cities and economies in ruin. Germany was divided and Japan became a democracy. The United Nations was created to promote peace and the decolonization of empires which then led to the beginning of the Cold War. Much of Asia and Europe was destoryed, prompting mass reconstruction efforts.
