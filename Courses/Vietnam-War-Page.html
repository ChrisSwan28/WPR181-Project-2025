The Origin of the Vietnam War

  • The war started in November 1st 1955 and ended on April 30th 1975
  • Vietnam wanted indpendance after French Colonialism. After World War 2 Vietnam was divided into Communist North Vs Anti-Communist South
  • The US supported the South while the USSR and China supported the North, thus the fear of the Domino Theory

The Key Events that took place during the war

  1. Geneva Accords divided Vietnam at the 17th parallel which was a temporary demarcation line between North and South (1954)
  2. The Gulf of Tonkin incident (1964)The US sent combat troops
  3. The Tet Offensive, this was the large North Vietnamese attack (1968)
  4. The My Lai Massacre, this was the mass murder of innocent civillians in South Vietnam (1968)
  5. The US began Vietnamisation in 1969 to 1973,this was when the US wass withdrawing
  6. The signing of the ParisPeace Accords which was the agreement of ending the war and restoring peace in Vietnam (1973)
  7. The fall of the Siagon which was when North Vietnam won the War (1975)

The Aftermath Of the Vietnam War

The war killed millions of Vietnamese civilians and soldiers, including approximately 58,000 American service members. The US military was demoralized, and the nation was bitterly split. Vietnam was reunited under communist leadership.
